Our company history

Graduate engineer Dieter Weiss founded his engineering office in 1971.

He ran the company as sole owner for almost 30 years. In 1998, his son Klaus Weiss joined his father as deputy. Since 2006, Klaus Weiss, a graduate engineer, has been the sole managing director.
